About Bart Simpson

Bartholomew JoJo "Bart" Simpson (born April 1 ,1979 or February 23, 1979) is the mischievous, rebellious, misunderstood, disruptive and "potentially dangerous" oldest child of the Simpson family in The Simpsons. He is the only son of Homer and Marge Simpson, and the older brother of Lisa and Maggie. Bart is known for his love for skateboarding, and rebellious attitude. Despite his antics, Bart has shown moments of intelligence and compassion, proving that there's more to him than just pranks. He attends Springfield Elementary School, where he often clashes with Principal Skinner and his teacher, Mrs. Krabappel.

Interests and Hobbies

Bart has a wide range of interests and hobbies that keep him busy and entertained. Here are some of the things he loves to do:

  • Skateboarding: Bart is rarely seen without his skateboard. He loves performing tricks and stunts all around Springfield.
  • Pranking: Known for his mischievous nature, Bart enjoys pulling pranks on his family, friends, and even his school principal.
  • Comics and Superheroes: Bart is an avid reader of comic books and has a particular fondness for the superhero Radioactive Man.
  • Video Games: Like many kids his age, Bart loves playing video games, especially the violent and action-packed ones.
  • Watching TV: Bart enjoys watching TV shows, with his favorites being Krusty the Clown and Itchy & Scratchy.

Despite his troublemaking ways, Bart's hobbies reflect his energetic and adventurous spirit.
