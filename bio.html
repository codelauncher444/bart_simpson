Who is Bart Simpson?

  1. Full Name: Bartholomew JoJo Simpson
  2. Age: 10 years old.
  3. Family: Son of Homer and Marge Simpson; brother to Lisa and Maggie.
  4. Personality: Mischievous, rebellious, and often disrespectful, but has a good heart.
  5. Best Friend: Milhouse Van Houten.
  6. School: Attends Springfield Elementary; often in trouble with Principal Skinner and teacher Mrs. Krabappel.
